SAVE OUR SCHOOL'S WIFI.

-- Welcome to SOSW. --

Our goal with SOSW is to provide free internet to our school. That means no more blocked websites, and we can access the internet outside of school securely.

-- Limitations --

Of course, SOSW doesn't come without limitations. SOSW is run on a repl with very little memory and processing power. Lots of games websites have crashed on our testing so don't expect it to be perfect.

Theres also the fact that the SOSW servers are not in the school. The servers are located somewhere around the world, and you just have to get used to it.

Also, SOSW is hosted on a linux-based system, which means it can't run things like Roblox and Steam*

 

*you can, but theres not enough processing power, and Roblox isn't officially supported on linux, so we're not trying that.
